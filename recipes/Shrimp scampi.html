Shrimp Scampi

Easy Peasy Shrimpy Scampi

This buttery lemon and garlic shrimp scampi can be served as a main course or an appetizer, making it a really versatile dish to have in your repertoire. I always serve this simple recipe for guests, and they tell me how gourmet and impressive it is every time.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • White wine
  • Lemon juice
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Thaw shrimp
  2. Melt half a stick of butter in a skillet
  3. Add shrimp into butter and cook until pink
  4. Add garlic and red pepper flakes
  5. Cook until fragrant
  6. Pour in wine and let it simmer until alcohol is boiled off
  7. Stir in the rest of the butter, lemon juice, and parsley
  8. Once the butter melts take it off the heat
  9. Season with salt and pepper as needed
  10. Serve with rice, couscous, or your pasta of choice
